At what age can I sign up my child for dance classes?

add_circle_outline remove_circle_outline

We offer Intro to Dance for 4- to 6-year-olds. This class introduces youngsters to basic dance vocabulary, teaches dance class etiquette, and develops coordination and strength.

What is the fastest way to become a better dancer?

add_circle_outline remove_circle_outline

The key to improving your dance skills is a well-rounded, balanced program that incorporates all aspects of dance instruction. We have a 3-part system: group classes, private lessons, and social dance parties. Utilizing all three will help you progress quickly, as each service compliments the other.

What should I wear for class?

add_circle_outline remove_circle_outline

Dancing is about movement and enjoyment. We recommend wearing comfortable clothing that allows you to be flexible and relaxed. You can wear anything you want for your first lesson. Afterwards, we will recommend something based on the style and direction you choose. Please avoid stilettos or shoes with sharp heels as those are not comfortable to dance in, and they may damage our floor.

Do you offer any specialized training?

add_circle_outline remove_circle_outline

Yes. We also offer specialized classes to complement what you are learning in your dance program, including presentation and styling.

Do you accept people with disabilities?

add_circle_outline remove_circle_outline

Absolutely! We believe everyone should dance. We accept and welcome people with disabilities. In fact, many of our instructors are certified to teach aspiring wheelchair dancers. We even work with children who have learning disabilities.

What is your cancellation policy?

add_circle_outline remove_circle_outline

We make every effort to accommodate the schedules of our students. We also work hard to retain the best teachers in the industry. In order to protect the time of our teachers, we have a 24-hour cancelation policy, so please let us know in advance of cancelations to avoid being charged.
